Step by Step to Get Connected to Our Pool

  • Use the link above to download XMR-stak. It is fairly easy to get installed on Windows and configures itself automatically.
  • Go to The QRL Wallet and generate yourself a new wallet. Please save your information in a safe place! This will be used to access your coin repository
  • When you start XMR-stak, choose "monero" as the coin you will mine. Don't worry you will be mining QRL as you connected to our pool. Next you will be alerted to input the pool address and port. Use the information above (in Connection Details and Mining Ports) to pick what software. The rest is not really necessary to worry about but I have pasted a sample config to follow. Mostly just answer false or blank.
    • "pool_list" : [ {"pool_address" : "strat.qrlmining.com:7777", "wallet_address" : "PUT YOUR WALLET ADDRESS", "pool_password" : "", "use_nicehash" : false, "use_tls" : false, "tls_fingerprint" : "", "pool_weight" : 1 }, ],
  • XMR-stak should begin mining. You will be able to log onto your wallet to check payouts -> we will be releasing tools on our site to better track specific mining stats before mainnet!
If you have any issues with the software or getting connected, please send an email over to info@qrlmining.com or join our Discord channel for help
